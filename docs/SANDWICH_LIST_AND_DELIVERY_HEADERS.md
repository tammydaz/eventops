## 4. DELIVERY BEO HEADERS — What Exists vs What’s Needed in Airtable

### Section headers on the Kitchen BEO print (delivery)

These are **labels in the code**, not Airtable fields. They are defined in `KitchenBEOPrintPage.tsx`:

| DESSERTS - DISPOSABLE | DESSERTS | flddPGfYJQxixWRq9 |

### Do you need new headers in Airtable?

**No.** The delivery BEO section headers (`HOT - DISPOSABLE`, `DELI - DISPOSABLE`, etc.) are **not stored in Airtable**. They are hardcoded in `DELIVERY_MENU_SECTION_CONFIG` in `KitchenBEOPrintPage.tsx`. The Events table already has the linked record fields (e.g. `DELIVERY_DELI`) that populate the content under each header.
